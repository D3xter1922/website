I read everything that lands in my inbox. Pick whichever channel fits best — and let me know how I can help.

## Preferred channels

- **Email:** [`hello@druhinabrol.com`](mailto:hello@druhinabrol.com) — updates, intros, or longer conversations.
- **LinkedIn:** [linkedin.com/in/druhinabrol](https://www.linkedin.com/in/druhinabrol) — professional updates and collaborations.
- **GitHub:** [github.com/druhinabrol](https://github.com/druhinabrol) — open-source work or code-related feedback.

If we already know each other, feel free to drop a message wherever we chat most often. Otherwise, email is the quickest way to get a thoughtful reply.

## What to include

- A little context about who you are.
- The problem you’re working on or the idea you want to explore.
- Any timelines, links, or constraints I should know about.

Looking forward to hearing from you — I try to respond within a couple of days.
