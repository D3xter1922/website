I’m treating this space like an evolving garden instead of a polished magazine. Some notes are seedlings, others are closer to harvest. Together they remind me that sharing early often leads to sharper thinking.

## Principles I’m trying to follow

- **Default to curiosity.** Capture questions before answers — it keeps ideas moving rather than locked into a take.
- **Prefer clarity over cleverness.** Jargon-free writing helps me understand what I truly mean.
- **Leave breadcrumbs.** Each note should point somewhere else worth exploring.

## What you’ll find

1. Working notes on systems thinking, tools for thought, and product development.
2. Retrospectives on projects: what worked, what didn’t, and what I’d try next.
3. Occasional deep-dives into the processes and rituals that keep me grounded.

Thanks for reading. I hope you find something useful — and if you do, tell me about it so I can keep iterating.
